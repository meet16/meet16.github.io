About Company Page


Introducing MUL’s flame proof and weather proof LED luminaire selection


MUL offers a vivid portfolio for selection of industrial luminaire that aims on effective heat dissipation along with wide angle and long range luminance using our patent design to provide best lighting results that lasts well then the conventional fluorescent and HID sources. Using MUL luminaire not only provides energy saying, but also long lasting power supply than traditional lighting solutions. MUL luminaire are the perfect solutions for a broad range of industrial and high bay lighting projects
